404 Error
Recipe (I mean) webpage not found
Back to Homepage

Ingredients:

1 cup of HTML

1 cup of CSS

1 tablespoon of JavaScript

A pinch of blue (#0077FF), gray (#CCCCCC), and black (#000000) for the color scheme


Directions:

Preheat your code editor to 404Â°F.

In a mixing bowl, combine the HTML and CSS until they form a smooth and cohesive mixture.

Add a tablespoon of JavaScript to the mixture and stir until fully combined.

Sprinkle in a pinch of blue (#0077FF) for the background color, gray (#CCCCCC) for the text color, and black (#000000) for the accent color, and mix until the colors are evenly distributed.

Pour the mixture onto a 404 page template and smooth it out with a spatula.

Bake in the browser for 10-15 seconds or until fully loaded.

Remove from the browser and allow the page to cool for a few seconds.

Serve with a side of helpful error messages and a link back to the homepage.

Enjoy your deliciously themed 404 page!
